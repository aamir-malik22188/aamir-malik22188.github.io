About Me

Hello and thank you for coming to my page.


My name is Aamir Malik and I am currently working at Inform Diagnostics (formerly known as Miraca Life Sciences) as an IT QA Tester/Trainer.


I have worked at Inform Diagnostics for the last 6 years and 5 of those 6 years, I was in a leadership role in Lab Operations.


I recently received my certification in Full Stack Development from Southern Methodist University (SMU). With this certification, I hope to develop and deliver meaningful applications that could help simplify certain tasks that we do on a day to day basis. However, I would also like to join a team and create impactful applications on a grander scale.


Now that you have read a little bit about me, please scroll down or click on the “Portofolio” link in the Navbar to see a few of the projects I have worked on.


Lastly, if you want to contact me, click on the “Contact Me” link in the Navbar or scroll to very bottom for direct links to my GitHub and Linkedin pages.
